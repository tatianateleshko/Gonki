                 Welcome in CarController Documentation:



SteeringWheel:
-This script is responsible for steering wheel revolutions

SpeedMeter:
-This script is responsible for speedMeter revolutions

RPM:
-This script is responsible for RPM arrow rotation

CarController:
-Thanks to Car Controller script car can drive.
In this script we have 10 elements to adjust. First four elements are concerning Tyre Colliders.
Another four means Tyres transforms. The last 2 elements are Max Steering Angle and Motor Force.

Camera Script:
-This script is responsible for camera movement during being in play mode. Object to follow means transform
of followed object. Offset determines start offset of camera. Then we have Follow speed and Lookspeed.
